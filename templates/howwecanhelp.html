Step 1

Identify

Learning how to identify the
early signs of mental health
issues can potentially decrease
their severity.
Formal treatment can help individuals manage
these issues as they pursue a
healthy future.The early signs
of mental health issues can
vary,depending on the specific
disorder, an individual’s circumstances, and other environmental factors.
-->You can personally identify youself by your own Thoughts,Likings,Opinions
and many more.All that matters is when you are YOU
Identify how you are feeling about this and then conquer it.

Step 2

Understand

Feeling this way makes you feel miserable and you might want to avoid the things which are actually helping you.
-->It’s important to understand that feeling depressed, anxious, lonely or showing anger is OK.
-->You need to understand that its OK to be in this situation and that it will pass very soon.
-->You will Get through this

Step 3

Learn

Talk to someone who’s already learned it.
--> Immerse yourself in the learning process.
--> Learn in short bursts.
--> Try to schedule your learning sessions in these short blocks, rather than aiming for occasional marathon sessions.
-->When learning something new, it’s helpful to ignore the fluff and focus purely on the most important elements of your subject.

Step 4

Practice

Once you know what the ways of overcoming these hurdles are, it is time for you to put them in practice mate!
-->Only keeping them in practice, will enhance results towards a happy living.
-->Continue implementing the principles and techniques throughout your life.
-->This provides you the opportunity to practice your new thinking and living on a daily basis.
-->This would further change the perspective of how you view things.

Step 5

Live Well

By the end of this guide,
you will be able to perceive things in a different way and change your behavioural patterns and will have taken the first steps towards becoming more engaged in your life.
You will be able to manage your thoughts, actions, and attention in more situations.
--> You will have the capability you truly need to live and live the life you always wanted.
