Loneliness is a universal human emotion that is both complex and unique to each individual. Loneliness, according to many experts, is not necessarily about being alone. Instead, if you feel alone and isolated, then that is how loneliness plays into your state of mind.

Suffering from loneliness is somewhat like suffering from physical pain. Loneliness can heighten the fight-or-flight response—the physiological reaction a person has when facing a threat.

Loneliness can be a risk factor for heart disease, Type 2 diabetes, arthritis, among other critical diseases.

Anxiety is a normal emotion. The American Psychological Association (APA) defines anxiety as “an emotion characterized by feelings of tension, worried thoughts and physical changes like increased blood pressure.”

Anxiety disorders affect 40 million people in the United States. It is the most common group of mental illnesses in the country.

But if you feel this often it might lead to disorders. Anxiety disorders form a category of mental health diagnoses that lead to excessive nervousness, fear, apprehension, and worry.

Know the difference between normal anxiety and anxiety that needs medical attention. It’s important to understand that anxiety disorders can be treated, even in severe cases.

Although, anxiety usually doesn’t go away, you can learn to manage it and live a happy, healthy life. Find out more about Anxiety on our page.

Depression is gradually becoming the most common mental illness in the world with the many number of people suffering from it, about 4% of the global population.

Depression leads to loss of interest in daily activities and extreme symptoms lead to self-harm and suicidal tendencies

Major depression is a treatable illness that affects the way a person thinks, feels, behaves, and functions .

The earlier that treatment can begin, the more effective it is. More than just about of the blues, depression isn't a weakness and you can't simply "snap out" of it.

Depression may require long-term treatment.
Diagnose today and seeking help is OK!

Insomnia is a sleep disorder in which you have trouble falling and/or staying asleep. The condition can be short-term (acute) or can last a long time (chronic).

It may also come and go. Acute insomnia lasts from 1 night to a few weeks. Insomnia is chronic when it happens at least 3 nights a week for 3 months or more.

What causes Insomnia ? Stress, Travel or work schedule, Poor sleep habits, Eating too much in the late evening, Over consumption of Caffeine, nicotine and alcohol.

. Insomnia risk factors include Long-term diseases or conditions like blood pressure and heart diseases, Mental health issues like depression , anxiety disorder etc.

Anger is a basic human emotion. But when it gets out of control and turns destructive, it can lead to problems—problems at work, in your personal relationships, and in the overall quality of your life.

And it can make you feel as though you're at the mercy of an unpredictable and powerful emotion

You need to know how to control it and when it turns to be a disorder. Prolonged release of the stress hormones that accompany anger can destroy neurons in areas of the brain associated with judgment and short-term memory, and weaken the immune system.

Letting anger issues go unaddressed can put your overall health at risk.

Some physical symptoms of anger-related problems include Tingling, Heart palpitations or tightening of the chest, Increased blood pressure, Headaches, Pressure in the head or sinus cavities, Fatigue. You can always test yourself or talk to experts. Control the anger before it controls you.

Our Story

ABHAYA was created in 2020 by a group of masters students who, through their personal work with multiple NGOs,realized that a more inclusive approach to mental health was needed.

After extensive research and consultation, ABHAYA was born.
ABHAYA is based on the premise that when an individual has a problem, they often seek help from their peers. Many individuals think their own family can't possibly understand what's happening in their lives and that their friends might laugh or abandon them. ABHAYA provides a safe, confidential place to talk things out with another fellow whose been in a similar situation who can understand and will listen, but not judge.

This simple act of listening can make a huge difference, sometimes a life-saving one.These individuals know they can trust ABHAYA - and they do.ABHAYA provides a safe space for such individuals to go ahead and be part of such more organisations and serve with love.
